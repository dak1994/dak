Restaurants And Bars

Indulge your palate with delicious international and local dishes passionately prepared with the freshest ingredients at Kuriftu Resort and Spa Afar. We offer indoor, alfresco and poolside dining with menus that promise delight in every bite. Start the day with a filling breakfast at ADD Restaurant. Or simply unwind after a day at our Sky Lounge, located on the 8th floor, which is the perfect place to relax and unwind with freshly squeezed juices at noon, signature cocktails at sunset and tasty snacks the whole day long with a breath-taking view of Samara. The four distinct venues take you on an extraordinary culinary journey, from authentic Asian cuisine at AsianBay to a vast buffet of Ethiopian and International flavours at ADD restaurant. In addition, our Connexions Lobby Lounge is the place to enjoy a quick coffee or leisurely cocktail, while Blu Jazz Bar is a vibrant social hub.

Dak lounge

Whether you are meeting colleagues or catching up with friends and family, the Dak lounge is the perfect option for guests who enjoy a delicious, sweet treat with their perfectly brewed coffee or tea.

A broad selection of refreshments including fresh juices and smoothies are also the perfect accompaniment to a delicious all-day menu that includes sandwiches, salads, burgers, and wraps.
